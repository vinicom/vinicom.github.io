FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is ViniCom Broadband?
    2. - ViniCom Broadband provides high speed fiber based broadband solution for both Home User and Business User. This Fiber based internet solution is delivered via an optical fibre directly to the home or office of the user. Through which users can enjoy high download and upload speeds, and also can operate multiple applications like Songs or Video downloads, online gaming or Video chat or web browsing and many more. 
     
  2. What are the advantages of Optical Fiber over copper cables?
    3. - Optical Fiber has many advantages than copper cable. Optical Fiber is able to transmit data much faster over greater distances than copper. As Optical Fiber carries pulses of light so chances of signal loss is minimal. Optical Fiber consumes less power (approx 36% lesser) than conventional copper cable line. Even your data is safe with Optical Fiber and very difficult to tap.
